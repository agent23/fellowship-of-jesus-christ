Getting Here

  • From Downtown: Take Main Street north for 2 miles, turn right on Faith Street
  • Public Transit: Bus Route 15 stops at Faith Street & Oak Avenue (2-block walk)

  • Accessibility: Handicapped parking and ramp access at the main entrance
