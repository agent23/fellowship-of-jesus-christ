About Fellowship of Jesus Christ

A community united in faith, hope, and love, serving God and our neighbors for over 70 years

Our Mission

To spread the love of Jesus Christ through authentic worship, meaningful fellowship, and compassionate service to our community and the world.

We believe that every person is created in God's image and has inherent worth and dignity. Our church family is committed to creating a welcoming environment where all can experience God's love and grace.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, making disciples who make disciples.

We envision a church where people from all walks of life can encounter Jesus Christ, grow in faith, and discover their unique calling to serve God and others.

Our Rich History

1952

Church Founded

Fellowship of Jesus Christ was established by a small group of devoted believers with a vision to serve the community.

1965

First Building

We built our first permanent sanctuary, providing a dedicated space for worship and fellowship.

1985

Community Outreach

Launched our first major community outreach programs, including a food pantry and youth programs.

2010

Expansion

Added our fellowship hall and education wing to accommodate our growing congregation.

2022

70th Anniversary

Celebrated seven decades of faithful service with over 300 active members and 15 ministry programs.
