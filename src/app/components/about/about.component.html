About Our Church

Founded in 1972, Fellowship of Jesus Christ has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for over decades. We are committed to creating an inclusive, loving environment where all can grow in their relationship with Christ.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ for the transformation of the world through worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, Christ-centered community that reflects God's love and grace while making a positive impact in our neighborhood and beyond.
